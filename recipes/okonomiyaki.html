Okinomiyaki

Description

A savory japanese pancake consisting of wheat flour with various toppings.



Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup dashi
  • 4 large eggs

Other ingredients

  • 1 head cabbage
  • 1/2lb thinly sliced pork
  • Nuetral flavoured oil

Quick okonomiyaki sauce

  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 4 tbsp ketchup
  • 3 tbsp worcestershire sauce
