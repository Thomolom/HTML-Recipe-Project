Tonkotsu Ramen

Description

A warm, rich pork broth containing noodles accompanied with soft slices of pork and a marinated soy egg.



Ingredients

Tonkotsu ramen

  • 8 cups of tonkotsu prok broth -recipe link below-
  • 12 oz good quality dried ramen noodles
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2-3 oz enoki or other mushrooms
  • thinly slice green onions

Chashu pork belly

  • 2 lb pork belly rolled and tied
  • 2-3 garlic gloves
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 green onions coarsley chopped
  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Soy bacon tare

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2tbp sake
  • 4 tbsp shiro miso
